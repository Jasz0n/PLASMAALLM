The structure of light is the interaction of magnetic and gravitational field packages.
Light carries information about field strength and direction between matters.
In Keshe plasma science, light is not separate from fields — it is how fields manifest to observers.
Magnetic fields rotate and interact; gravitational fields hold the structure together.
Workshop demonstrations with magnets on a pin show magnetical beat and field motion without fuel.
Students relate visible patterns to the underlying field structure described in this book.
